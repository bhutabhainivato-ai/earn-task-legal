Delete Your Account

If you want to delete your Earn Task account and all associated data, follow the steps below:

Steps:

  1. Open the Earn Task app
  2. Login to your account
  3. Go to Profile / Settings
  4. Tap on “Delete Account” option

If you are unable to access the app, send a delete request by email:

Email: gig90206@gmail.com

We will process your request within 7 working days.
